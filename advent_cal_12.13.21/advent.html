12 Days of Holiday Fun

Here's to the start of a beautiful season!

Bake some cookies for friends! Just don't eat me.

Give your family a hug!

Take a night drive to see the holiday lights in your city or town.

If you have the means, consider a donation to a local shelter (human or animal).

Start a new tradition by making your own ornament or buy one to exchange with family or friends.

Deck your halls with popcorn garland or felt poms!

Cut your own xmas tree down or a potted rosemary plant to trim your tree.

Make the most of the remaining time this year, think of one thing that made you content.

Be sweet! Others will notice.

Consider volunteering your time to those in need this season.

Light up the night with your beautiful smile!
